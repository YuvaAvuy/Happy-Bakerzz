Quality and trust

Quality and trust are at the heart of Happy bakers, and have helped the company delight its customers for close to four decades. The classic Happy bakers products are produced at a centralised, state-of-the-art, air-conditioned kitchen spread over more than two lakh sq feet (20,000 sq metres) of land in neelambur, a suburb in coimbatore. Around 30 workers work round the clock on more than 500 different types of food items, including bakery menus and bulk party and event orders. The company has also started production and sale of milk and yoghurt under the Happy bakers brand.
